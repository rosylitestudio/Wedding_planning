# Wedding_planning
A beautiful, fully-functional wedding planning tool built with pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. All data stays on your device - no accounts, no subscriptions, no cloud uploads.
✨ Features
19 Complete Planning Tabs
Setup — Couple names, wedding date, location, photo
Dashboard — Overview with charts and progress tracking
Guest List — RSVP tracking, dietary preferences, contact info
Seating Plan — Table assignments and arrangements
Vendors — Track all vendors, costs, and status
Budget — Real-time spending tracker with categories
Venue & Food — Catering planning and calculations
Timeline — Key milestones with countdown
Beauty Appointments — Makeup, hair, nails, and other services
Bride Checklist — Personal planning tasks
Groom Checklist — His tasks to keep him involved
Contacts — All vendor and family phone numbers/emails
Wedding Day Itinerary — Minute-by-minute schedule
Music & Playlist — Songs organized by category
Honeymoon — Trip planning and expense tracking
Packing List — Travel checklist with categories
Photo Gallery — Upload wedding inspiration and mood boards
Photography — Photographer details and must-have shots
Cake Tasting — Track flavors, costs, and final choice
